General Rules

1. You must pay $5 to participate.
2. You can "kill" your target only
3. For a valid "kill" submit a video of the assassination to the Instagram page.
4. Kills may not be made in safe zones.
5. The game continues until one assassin remains.
6. When you are killed, you are out of the game.
7. Do not tell anyone who your target is.
8. Once kill is authenticated, next target will be assigned. The next target will be the person you just killed's target
9. You must kill target two weeks after being assigned. Failure to do so will result in elimination.
10. No teaming.
11. You must be a Homestead Senior to play.

Weapons

1. Nerf Guns or any off brand gun. Must have foam darts. The dart must make contact with any part of the person. Please avoid head and eye shots.
2. Grenades Water balloons are allowed outside as long as the target is Not Visibly Carrying Sensitive Material (Papers, Books, Electronics, Etc.) . The water balloon must hit the person and explode.

Safe Zones

1. Any where on school campus (Including the student parking lot and athletic fields).
2. Any school affiliated activites off school campus(sports games, club meetings)
3. Places of work or worship.
4. If you strip down to your underwear you are safe regarless if you are in public.
5. Ten minute grace period before and after the school starts.
6. No trespassing, don't break into houses, don't do dumb shit. Use your common sense.
7. Inside private establishments and stores.
